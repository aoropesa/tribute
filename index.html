Here's a timeline of Shakira's Life:

  • 1977: Shakira Isabel Mebarak Ripoll was born February 2 in Barranquilla, Colombia.
  • 1990: She failed to attain commercial success in her first studio albums, Magia and Peligro.
  • 2002: Shakira entered the English-language market with her fifth album, Laundry Service. Its lead single, "Whenever, Wherever", became the best-selling single of 2002.
  • 2004: Her tenth album, Shakira, is preceded by its lead single, "Can't Remember to Forget You".
  • 2005: Shakira became the first artist to perform a Spanish language song at the MTV Video Music Awards.
  • Shakira has won many awards, including five MTV Video Music Awards, two Grammy Awards, eight Latin Grammy Awards, seven Billboard Music Awards, 28 Billboard Latin Music Awards and has been Golden Globe-nominated.
  • She is singer, songwriter, dancer, and record producer.
  • She has a star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame, and has sold between 70 and 125 million records worldwide.
  • 2014: she was listed as the 58th most powerful woman in the world by Forbes.
